Text

"Dabheoc's name is commemorated, too, in Seadavog mountain, a low peak to the west of his Seat on the southern shore of the lake. Seadavog was likewise the ancient name of the townland from which this mountain rises. Ballymacavanny is its modern name, but Seadavog still clings to one small part of the townland, its western end."
