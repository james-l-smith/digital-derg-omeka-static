Text

“Part by this race when monks in convents of coracles For the Merovingian centuries left their land, Belled, fragrant; and honest in their oracles Bespoke the grace to give without demand Martyrs Heaven winged nor tempted with reward. And not ours, doughed in dogma, who never have dared”
