Text

"Here are also several bogs, moors &c. but not dignified with names. The country formerly was over-run with forests, of which at present there are but small remains ; of these, the woods of Tawnawanny and Mucruss are the most remarkable. There have not as yet been seen, any plants or herbs peculiar to the country."
