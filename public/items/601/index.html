Text

"I departed from that king's court and followed the road for several days — because the road is long — until I reached one of their towns called Tearmann and they call it thus because there they will do no harm to anyone; it would seem they have great devotion to Saint Patrick and it is good through the realm; and the kings keep that town safe and the pilgrims who go there are obliged to leave their animals there for horses or other animals could not cross the mountains or the waters. "
