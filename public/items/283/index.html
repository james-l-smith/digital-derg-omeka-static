Text

“We pray to ourself. The metal moon, unspent Virgin eternity sleeping in the mind, Excites the form of prayer without content; Whitehorn lightens, delicate and blind, The negro mountain, and so, knelt on her sod, This woman beside me murmuring My God! My God!”
