Text

"There are 4 small rivers that run through the parish. The Pettigoe-river, the Omna, the Letter-river. The Pettigoe river rises about 5 miles north of Pettigoe, and runs in a southerly direction ; about a mile north of Pettigoe, it is joined by the Omna, which rises near Lough Derg, and runs in a south-easterly direction ; after their junction the united stream passes through Pettigoe, and falls into Lough Erne, about a mile south of Pettigoe."
