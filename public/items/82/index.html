Title

"That's the Red Lake unless I mistake"

Subject

Lough Derg--Pilgrimage--Depiction--Poetry

Description

"Now it seems there's a place they call Purgat're, so I must write it, my verse not admitting the O. But as for the venue I vow I'm perplext To say it's in this world or if in the next, Or whether in both for 'tis very well known That St Patrick at least has got one of his own In a tight little Island that stands in Lake Call'd Lough Derg : that's the Red Lake unless I mistake."
